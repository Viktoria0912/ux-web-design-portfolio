About Me

I'm a UX designer and web developer with a passion for creating beautiful, functional digital experiences. With a background in both design and development, I bridge the gap between aesthetics and functionality.

My approach combines user-centered design principles with clean, efficient code to create solutions that are both visually appealing and technically sound. I believe that great design should be intuitive, accessible, and purposeful.

My Design Process

01. Research

Understanding user needs, business goals, and market context through in-depth research and analysis.

02. Define

Synthesizing research insights to define the core problems and establish clear design objectives.

03. Ideate

Exploring multiple creative solutions through sketching, wireframing, and collaborative brainstorming.

04. Prototype

Building interactive prototypes to test concepts, interactions, and user flows.

05. Test

Evaluating designs with real users to gather feedback and identify areas for improvement.

06. Implement

Bringing designs to life through clean, efficient code that performs seamlessly across devices.
